# 🎓 SQL Project - University Management System

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing a **University Management System database** using SQL.  
The goal is to extract meaningful insights about **students, courses, professors, and academic performance** to support better decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze student academic behaviour and performance  
- Evaluate course popularity and workload distribution  
- Understand professor and department contributions  
- Identify academic trends and patterns  
- Perform predictive analysis for student success  

## 🗂️ Database Components
The analysis is based on the following key entities:
- Students  
- Courses  
- Enrollments  
- Professors  
- Departments  
- Grades  

## 📊 Analysis & Insights

### 👨‍🎓 Student Analysis

#### 1. Students with Highest Course Enrollment  
**Insight:** Students enrolled in the highest number of courses show **high academic engagement** and motivation for broader learning.

#### 2. Consistently High-Performing Students (A/B Grades)  
**Insight:** These students demonstrate **strong academic consistency** and are ideal candidates for **merit-based scholarships**.

#### 3. Average Grade per Student  
**Insight:**  
- Identifies **top performers**  
- Highlights **students needing academic support**  
- Useful for **personalized counseling and mentoring**

## 📚 Course & Enrollment Analysis

#### 1. Top 5 Most Enrolled Courses  
**Insight:**  
Popular courses likely:
- Have **high career relevance**  
- Are **core subjects**  
- Or are perceived as **easier to score**

#### 2. Low Enrollment Courses  
**Insight:**  
Low demand may indicate:
- Lack of awareness  
- High difficulty level  
- Low practical relevance  

#### 3. Average Credits per Semester  
**Insight:**  
- Balanced credits → **Healthy workload**  
- High credits → **Risk of student stress/burnout**

## 👨‍🏫 Professor & Department Insights

#### 1. Professors Teaching Most Courses  
**Insight:** Indicates **Higher workload** OR **high student preference/popularity**

#### 2. Departments with Most Professors  
**Insight:**  Shows **resource allocation** and should align with **student enrollment size**.

#### 3. Professors with Highest Average Grades  
**Insight:**  
Could indicate: **Effective teaching methods** OR **lenient grading**  

## 📈 Academic Performance & Trends

#### 1. Average Grade by Department  
**Insight:** Departments with higher averages may reflect: **Better teaching quality**  OR **easier evaluation patterns**

#### 2. Most Difficult Courses  
**Insight:** Courses with low average grades may need Curriculum improvement Better teaching support  

#### 3. Grade Trends Over Time  
**Insight:**  
- Improvement → **Better learning/adaptation**  
- Decline → **Possible gaps in teaching or curriculum**

## 🔍 Comparative & Predictive Analysis

#### 1. Department Enrollment Growth  
**Insight:**  Fast-growing departments indicate **High market demand** **Student interest trends**

#### 2. At-Risk Students Prediction  
**Insight:**  Students with Low grades & High course load.

#### 3. Credits vs Grades Relationship  
**Insight:**  Positive correlation shows Students manage workload well & Negative correlation show Overloading impacts performance.
